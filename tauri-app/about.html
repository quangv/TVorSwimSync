TVorSwimSync

Version 0.1.0
About

TVorSwimSync automatically syncs stock symbols between TradingView and thinkorswim.

Features

• Auto-sync symbols when you change charts in TradingView
• One-click manual sync
• Position calibration for accuracy

⚠ Beta Software
This is beta software. Use at your own risk and always verify symbol accuracy before trading.
Getting Started

1. Use "Setup Auto-Sync Target..." to calibrate the symbol field location
2. Enable "Auto-Sync Enabled (Beta)" in the menu
3. Change symbols in TradingView to sync automatically
